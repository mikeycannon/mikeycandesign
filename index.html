Bizimply

Co-founder & Head of Product


Bizimply.com

I joined Bizimply as a co-founder and Head of Product. My role is impossible to describe but ultimately my focus was on creating simple solutions to complex problems for hospitality and retail businesses. I worked on all teams in Bizimply to ensure that we created solutions that make a difference to our customers - and to ensure we actually got customers! Bizimply is a workforce management platform that allows businesses to increase productivity, improve employee engagement while lowering and maintaining labour costs through scheduling, attendance and more.
I left Bizimply in 2019 to gain more experience working with larger design and product teams. During my 6 years with Bizimply we went from 0 to 500+ clients, €1M+ in annual revenues, 20+ staff and raised over €2.5M in funding. It was an amazing journey for me and Bizimply is still going strong today.

Final Year Project - Frame

Frame was a ladder designed to promote safe practices and actively discourage dangerous or improper use. It allowed users to stay balanced and supported at all times with ample room for various tools required to complete any task. Ergonomics and user behavior were a key aspect of study in this project along with exploring manufacturing options including rotational molding and material selection.
